📝 Tales of Chonkus

“The Pastry Incident”

Chonkus once fell asleep inside a bakery display. Tourists thought he was a new kind of croissant. He did not move, but he did gain three followers.

“The Fish Ultimatum”

A priest once tried to ignore Chonkus during mass. The cat stared for six minutes straight. The priest broke, handed him a fish, and resumed the sermon.

“The Olive Oil Heist”

Caught licking an unattended olive oil bottle, Chonkus looked up slowly... and continued licking. The owner apologized for interrupting.

“Siesta Siege”

He once occupied a restaurant chair for seven consecutive hours. No one dared move him. The restaurant now calls it “The Throne.”

“Ghost Meower of Knossos”

Locals swear they hear him meowing late at night from inside ancient ruins. Historians say it’s impossible. Chonkus says nothing.

“The Scooter Standoff”

A delivery scooter beeped at Chonkus to move. Chonkus blinked once. The driver reversed 10 meters and took a different route.

“The Belly Eclipse”

During a solar eclipse, someone looked up, then down — and realized it was just Chonkus rolling over in the sun.

“The Gyro Gambit”

He once approached a tourist, placed a paw on their gyro, and meowed softly. They gave him the whole thing. He nodded once, then left.

“Purrliament of Cats”

Five other cats once gathered around Chonkus in a circle. No one moved. After five minutes, they all dispersed in different directions. No one knows what was decided.

“The Yelp Review”

A taverna got a five-star Google review saying, “Excellent food, plus cat.” The owner didn’t have a cat. Chonkus had simply appeared that day.
